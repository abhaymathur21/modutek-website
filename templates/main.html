ABOUT US

At Modutek Systems, we manufacture & develop modular workstations, institutional, residential and new-age innovations in furniture. In today's fast-moving world, modular furniture provides us with the flexibility of design, building it from scratch to better suit any space constraint and environment.

Founded in the bustling city of Mumbai with 15 years of experience, Modutek Systems has swiftly risen to prominence as a premier manufacturer of modular furnitures.
